How It Works

A simple process designed to keep premium tailoring clear.

From selecting a suit to submitting professional measurements, each step is built to make custom menswear more approachable.

01

Choose your suit

Select the collection, tone, and silhouette that match your event, personal style, and budget.

02

Decide your configuration

Choose between a 2-piece suit, a 3-piece upgrade, or a premium fabric upgrade based on the look you want.

03

Get professionally measured

Visit a local tailor or seamstress so your sizing is accurate and the final garment fits as intended.

04

Submit and complete your order

Upload your completed measurement sheet after purchase so NECKTIE can begin production on your tailored suit.
