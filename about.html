About NECKTIE

Tailoring built to make premium suiting more accessible.

NECKTIE exists to bring cleaner fit, sharper styling, and elevated menswear to clients who want custom tailoring without unnecessary complexity.

Brand Story

Why we started

Too many men are forced to choose between expensive bespoke tailoring and generic off-the-rack fits. NECKTIE was created to close that gap with a simpler, more refined path to a tailored suit.

Mission

What we believe

Premium tailoring should feel clear, sharp, and approachable. Our mission is to combine elevated styling with straightforward ordering and fit guidance.

Values

What matters

  • Clean design and confident fit
  • Accessible pricing without losing polish
  • Professional measurements for better outcomes
  • Menswear that feels intentional and timeless
